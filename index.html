Executive Overview

Client Context

6–12 months

Greencore is a leading UK manufacturer of convenience foods with 16 manufacturing sites, 618 direct-to-store vehicles[AR2024], and ~1,600 SKUs. FY23 revenue was £1,913.7m with an OEE and operational reset under way as part of Horizon 2 (Rebuild).

FY24 highlights: Adjusted Operating Profit £97.5m[AR2024], Operating Profit £84.3m[AR2024], Free Cash Flow £70.1m[AR2024], ROIC 11.5%[AR2024].

This proposal synthesises Greencore’s FY23 priorities (Operational Excellence reset; systems upgrades; Better Future Plan focus on waste, energy, healthy diets, and communities) and maps AI initiatives to COGS, Distribution/Fleet, and SG&A.

Key Challenges

  • Variable OEE and yield loss across sites; seasonal demand spikes
  • Fuel & route inefficiency across 10,500 daily deliveries[AR2024]
  • High innovation tempo with health & sustainability constraints
  • Margin pressure; need for pricing & promo optimisation with retailers
  • Energy-intensive chilled footprint; need to halve food waste
  • Heavy document & contract load across procurement, QA, and legal

Success Factors

  • ERP/MES/TMS/WMS integrations with clean master data
  • Operator & planner upskilling; clear KPI governance
  • Privacy-by-design and robust MLOps (monitoring & drift)
  • Phased rollout with site champions and control towers

2.1 Manufacturing & Operational Efficiency – AI Deployment

Main Results

  • +15–20% OEE within 6–12 months on targeted lines
  • Yield loss -1.5–2pp (~£3M p.a.)
  • Downtime -30–40% (~£2M–£3M productivity gains)
  • Labour optimisation: redeploy/reduce 50–70 FTEs
  • £6M–£8M p.a. savings; payback < 2 years; ROI > 300%

Approach & Key Success Factors

  • Predictive maintenance (vibration/thermal + model-based)
  • Computer vision QC for sandwiches, salads, ready meals
  • AI scheduling to balance SKU demand vs labour & machines
  • Unified AI-OEE dashboard integrated with MES/ERP
  • Operator enablement & change management

Quick Wins (≤ 6 months)

High-impact pilots that can be delivered in under six months using existing data and infrastructure.

  • Fresh Food Demand Sensing (SKU x Channel) ≤6 months
    Pilot gradient-boosted + lightweight LLM features for promo/weather; target: forecast MAE ↓ 10–20% and waste ↓ 5–10% (modelled impact; will be validated in pilot).
  • Computer Vision for Quality (inline defect detection) ≤6 months
    Edge cameras on 1–2 lines; target: false rejects ↓ and giveaway ↓ 1–2pp; improves yield and consistency (modelled).
  • Transport Re-optimisation (DTS) ≤6 months
    Dynamic route/slotting with live constraints; target: miles ↓ 3–5% and on-time ↑ (modelled). Fleet size 618 vehicles[AR2024] and ~10,500 daily deliveries[AR2024] provide leverage.
  • Cash & Working Capital Forecasting ≤6 months
    Time-series + heuristic features to stabilise weekly cash forecast and inventory turns; enables earlier interventions (modelled).
